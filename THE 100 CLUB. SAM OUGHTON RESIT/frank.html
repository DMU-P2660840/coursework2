He WAS known for his scoring ability and holds the record for being the all-time top scorer for his club, with 211 goals to his name in all competitions. He has been hailed by many renowned opponent team managers as the best player in the world, including José Mourinho and Luiz Felipe Scolari. After leaving Chelsea, he joined the NYCFC team to play in the US Major League Soccer. He has also written an autobiography, titled 'Totally Frank', and a series of children's novels detailing the adventures of the protagonist Frankie with his magic football. Frank Lampard was named as a coach of Derby County in May 2018. In the 2018–19 Championship season, the team finished in sixth place and reached the Championship play-offs. In July 2019, Frank Lampard was named as a Chelsea head coach, becoming Chelsea's first English coach after two decades.
